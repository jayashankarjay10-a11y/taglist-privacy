What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

The Application acquires the information you supply when you interact with its features. Registration is not mandatory. The information processed includes:

  • Player Logs: Names or identifiers of players you choose to track.
  • Behavioral Data: Tags (e.g., Friendly, Dirty, Neutral) and numerical ratings you assign to players based on your interactions.
