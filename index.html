Privacy Policy

This privacy policy is applicable to the TagList app (hereinafter referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, which was developed by Jay (hereinafter referred to as "Service Provider") as an Ad Supported service. This service is provided "AS IS".

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

The Application acquires the information you supply when you interact with its features. The information processed includes:

  • Player Logs & Behavioral Data: Names, tags, and ratings you assign to players.
  • Game Management: Names and custom logos you associate with the games you track.
  • Scanned Text: Information extracted via the camera scanning feature.

Camera and Image Processing

The Application features a Text Recognition Scanner to help you quickly input player names.

  • Camera Access: The Application requests permission to access your device's camera solely for the purpose of detecting text on the screen (e.g., scanning a player's name from a game lobby).
  • Real-time Processing: Camera frames are processed in real-time. The Application does not save, store, or transmit your camera's photos or video frames to any server.

Logo Uploads and Storage

The Application allows you to personalize your dashboard by adding logos to your games.

  • Media Access: If you choose to add a logo, the Application will request access to your photo gallery via the system's standard photo picker.
  • Local Storage Only: When you select a logo, the Application creates a cropped, optimized copy of that image and saves it in the Application's private internal storage on your device.
  • No Cloud Uploads: These images are never uploaded to a server, shared with third parties, or made accessible to other apps.

Secure Backup & Restore (Premium Feature)

The Application provides a local backup system to ensure your data is portable and safe from accidental loss.

  • AES Encryption: All backup files are automatically encrypted using Advanced Encryption Standard (AES) logic. This ensures that your player notes and ratings are private and cannot be read by any other application or text editor.
  • Local Control: Backups are saved only to the location you choose on your device or linked cloud storage (like Google Drive). The Service Provider never has access to your backup files.
  • Selective Export: You have the power to choose exactly which games and player lists are included in your backup files.

Does the Application use Artificial Intelligence (AI) or Automated Analytics?

The Application uses automated data processing to deliver unique insights:

  • OCR Technology: Uses local machine learning to convert camera images into text.
  • Community Health Scoring: Automatically processes your ratings and tags to rank gaming communities from "Safest" to "Highest Toxicity."
  • Behavioral Recognition: Analyzes player tags to identify common community behaviors and trends.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information?

Only aggregated, anonymized data is periodically transmitted to external services to aid in improving the Application. Your private player logs, game logos, and camera data are never shared. The Application utilizes third-party services with their own Privacy Policies:

  • Google Play Services

You can use the "Reset All Data" feature in the App Settings to permanently delete all your tags, games, players, and saved logos from your device.
